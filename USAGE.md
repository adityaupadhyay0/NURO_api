# 📖 Usage Guide: NeuroMark Pro 10x

Welcome to the command center. This guide explains how to use the different modules of the platform to achieve 10x marketing results.

---

## 🤖 1. The Campaign Brain (AaaS)
The **Campaign Brain** is your central portal.
- **How to use**: Type a natural language goal into the chat input (e.g., *"How do I launch a high-ROAS campaign for my supplement brand on TikTok?"*).
- **What happens**: The `BrainOrchestrator` calls the **Creative Strategist** and **Media Buyer** agents. They analyze your goal and provide a coordinated plan.
- **1-Click Execute**: Use the execution button to move the plan into action (e.g., generating hooks or spying on competitors).

## 🚀 2. Predict & Optimize (The core)
Use this to analyze a specific asset before you go live.
1. **Target Audience**: Select your age, platform, industry, and awareness level. This is *critical* for deterministic calibration.
2. **Upload**: Drop a video, audio file, or text script.
3. **Analyze**: Click "Predict Winning Probability."
4. **Insights**:
   - **Winning Probability**: A single score showing how likely you are to win the auction.
   - **KPI Timeline**: See exactly where users drop off (Clarity) or spike (Attention).
   - **Heatmaps**: View the visual attention density at the most critical "Moment of Impact."

## 📊 3. Batch Creative Ranking
Don't guess which variant of your ad is better.
- **Upload up to 100 variants** (e.g., different hooks, different background music).
- The system will process them in high-throughput mode and provide a **Ranked Leaderboard**.
- Use the **Export CSV** button to share the rankings with your production team.

## 🕵️ 4. Competitor Neuro-Spy
- **Input any URL** (Competitor landing page, YouTube ad link, etc.).
- The system scrapes the headline, content, and CTAs.
- The **Neuro-Analyst** agent decodes their "Secret Sauce" and emotional hooks, while the **CRO Optimizer** provides a predictive report on how to beat their conversion rate.

## ✍️ 5. AI Hook Generator
- Provide a product description and target audience.
- Our **10x Creative Strategist** (trained on millions in ad spend) generates 5-10 high-performance hooks designed to spike the "Scroll-Stop Rate" based on the neural profiles of your audience.

## ⚔️ 6. Creative Battle Royale
- Side-by-side comparison of two ad variants.
- Visualizes the "Head-to-Head" neural resonance. The winner is the ad that maximizes the **Winning Probability** across the audience segment.

## 📉 7. Prediction vs. Reality (Feedback Loop)
- Once your campaign is live, enter your **Real CTR** and **Real CPA**.
- The system calculates the **Variance**.
- Use this to calibrate the AI. The agents will see your previous variances and adjust their future creative advice to match your specific account performance.

---

## 💡 Pro Tips for 10x Results
- **Awareness Level Matters**: "Cold" audiences need higher Attention/Hook scores, while "Hot" audiences respond better to "Reward" (offer-based) spikes.
- **Watch the 'Clarity' Score**: If Clarity drops below 40%, your message is too complex for the platform (especially on TikTok).
- **Creative Fatigue**: If the Fatigue Index is over 80, your ad will likely "die" within 7 days. Plan your next creative batch immediately.
